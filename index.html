What does Spanner do?

On opening the app, Spanner accepts information regarding your car such as the brand and model as well as whatever problem you think you might be facing. On submitting this information, the app returns a list of nearby car repair services-having picked up your location automatically-and ranks them according to their proximity to you.

From there you-as a user- are free to select ehich sevice you would like to use. Choice can be made based on procimity and the provided rating information. This comes in form of a row of stars below each service's name.
